Campus Info: Casa Loma campus has five buildings and is situated on the stretch of Kendal Avenue between Davenport Road and MacPherson Avenue.
Student Life: It is pretty good, although not having a co-op opportunity for us is a major downside, but we will persevere!
Casa Loma: in 1973, the Casa Loma campus was added to GBC's ownership and provided programs in construction, computer systems and network, fashion(clothing, jewerly, accessories, etc) and dance! The goal was to provide programs for people that wanted to study in Technology and Arts.
