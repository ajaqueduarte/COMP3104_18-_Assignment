COMP 3104 is a DevOps course that delves into the world of Development and Operations collaboration in software development. In this course, students explore key DevOps concepts and practices, learning to automate processes, integrate continuous integration and deployment (CI/CD) pipelines, monitor systems in real-time, and ensure security throughout the software development lifecycle. They gain hands-on experience with essential DevOps tools and technologies like Git, Docker, and Kubernetes. Through practical case studies, students understand how to foster effective teamwork, maintain scalability, and ensure high availability in modern software environments. Overall, COMP 3104 equips students with the expertise to streamline and optimize software development and deployment, making them valuable contributors in the DevOps landscape.
